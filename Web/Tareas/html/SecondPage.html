Frogs have specialized skin that allows them to breathe through it, making them one of the few animals that can absorb oxygen both through their lungs and their skin.

Frogs can jump incredibly long distances for their size, with some species able to leap over 20 times their body length!

Frogs have a unique way of eating: They use their eyes to help swallow food. When they swallow, their eyes sink into their skulls to push the food down their throat.

Frogs have no tails as adults, but they do have tails when they are tadpoles. Their tails disappear during metamorphosis.
